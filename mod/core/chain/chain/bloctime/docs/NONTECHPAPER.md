# BlocTime: Simple Guide for Everyone

## What is BlocTime?

BlocTime is like a digital savings account that rewards you for locking up your tokens. The longer you lock them, the more bonus tokens you earn!

## How Does It Work?

### 1. **Staking (Locking Your Tokens)**
- You deposit your tokens into BlocTime
- Choose how long to lock them (measured in blocks)
- Get BlocTime tokens as a reward
- **The longer you lock = The more you earn!**

**Example:** Lock 100 tokens for 1000 blocks, get 120 BlocTime tokens. Lock for 5000 blocks, get 200 BlocTime tokens!

### 2. **The Marketplace**
- Buy stable tokens using approved cryptocurrencies
- Prices are set fairly using trusted sources
- Simple credit/debit system
- Your stable tokens are always worth the same

**Example:** Pay with ETH to get stable tokens you can use anywhere in the system.

### 3. **The Treasury (Shared Vault)**
- Hold BlocTime tokens = Own part of the treasury
- Treasury collects different types of tokens
- Claim your share anytime
- **Own 10% of BlocTime tokens = Get 10% of ALL treasury tokens!**

**Example:** Treasury has ETH, USDC, and DAI. You own 5% of BlocTime tokens, so you can claim 5% of each token type!

### 4. **The Registry (Digital Catalog)**
- Register your digital assets
- Store information about them
- Transfer ownership easily
- Keep track of everything you own

**Example:** Register your NFT collection, game items, or any digital asset with metadata.

### 5. **Permissions (Access Control)**
- Create keys that control access
- Give different permissions to different people
- Manage who can do what
- Keep your assets secure

**Example:** Create a master key for your project, then give team members specific access keys.

## Important Terms (Simple)

- **Block**: A unit of time on the blockchain (~15 seconds)
- **Staking**: Locking tokens to earn rewards
- **Multiplier**: Bonus percentage for longer locks
- **Treasury**: Shared vault of tokens
- **Governance Token**: Token that gives you voting/claiming rights
- **Oracle**: Trusted price information source
